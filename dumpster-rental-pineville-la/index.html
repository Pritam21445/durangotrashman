Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know what size dumpster I need?

The best way is to call us and describe your project. As a general guide, a 10 yard dumpster is for small projects, a 20 yard dumpster is for medium-sized renovations and cleanouts, and a 30 or 40 yard dumpster is for major construction or whole-home gutting.

Is it better to rent a dumpster or hire a junk removal service?

A dumpster rental in Pineville, LA provides you with a container for a set period, allowing you to work at your own pace. A junk removal service typically involves a crew that loads and hauls everything at once. A dumpster is often the more cost-effective option for larger projects.

Do I need to be present for the delivery or pickup?

No, you do not need to be on-site, provided that the delivery area is clear, accessible, and you have given us specific instructions on where to place the roll off dumpster container.

What can I not put in the dumpster?

You cannot dispose of any hazardous materials. This includes paints, chemicals, oils, fuels, asbestos, tires, batteries, and appliances containing Freon.

Will the dumpster damage my driveway?

While we are extremely careful, a heavy steel bin can potentially scuff a surface. We strongly recommend placing sheets of plywood down on your driveway before the container is delivered to protect the surface.
